today i fixed the bug where math formulas can't be rendered.

Now, MathJax has been integrated properly, and you can see math formulas like $E = mc^2$ inline or using display math:

$$
\int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, dx = F(b) - F(a)
$$

This fix will make reading and writing math on my blog a lot more enjoyable for everyone!
